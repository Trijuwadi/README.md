## Annotations

### 1. Aleyda Solis
Aleyda is one of the most respected international SEO consultants. She runs #SEOFOMO, a weekly newsletter covering SEO news and AI developments. Her content is highly practical — she shares frameworks, not opinions. She was one of the first to write seriously about how AI content affects E-E-A-T and Google's quality signals.

### 2. Lily Ray
Lily is VP of SEO Strategy at Amsive and is known for data-heavy analysis of Google algorithm updates. She has produced some of the most cited research on how AI-generated content performs in search. She bridges the gap between "what Google says" and "what the data shows."

### 3. Kevin Indig
Kevin is a growth advisor who previously led SEO at Shopify and G2. He writes the Growth Memo on Substack, which regularly covers the intersection of AI tools and content strategy. His experience at large-scale SaaS companies makes his frameworks directly applicable.

### 4. Kyle Roof
Kyle is the co-founder of PageOptimizer Pro. He's known for running controlled SEO experiments — testing variables like keyword placement, semantic density, and content structure in isolation. His approach is empirical, not anecdotal, which makes his content uniquely valuable.

### 5. Wil Reynolds
Wil is the founder of Seer Interactive, one of the original data-driven SEO agencies. He is vocal about how AI is changing SEO workflows at the agency level. His content often challenges conventional wisdom with real data.

### 6. Cyrus Shepard
Cyrus is the founder of Zyppy and a former Moz team member. He is known for publishing original research on topics like internal linking, title tag optimization, and content quality signals. His findings are frequently cited across the SEO community.

### 7. Eli Schwartz
Eli is the author of "Product-Led SEO," which has become a foundational text for SaaS companies building organic growth programs. As a consultant, he has worked with Quora, Zendesk, and other major SaaS companies. His framework challenges the "more content = more traffic" assumption.

### 8. Brendan Hufford
Brendan runs Growth Sprints and is one of the clearest voices on AI-powered SEO specifically for SaaS companies. He shares detailed case studies and frameworks from his consulting work, making his content highly actionable.

### 9. Ryan Law
Ryan is the Director of Content at Ahrefs. His writing on content strategy and SEO is among the most rigorous in the industry. He focuses on the "why content fails" question, which is critical context for AI content production.

### 10. Gaetano DiNardi
Gaetano is a growth advisor who has held senior marketing roles at Nextiva, Pipedrive, and Sales Hacker. He shares detailed, B2B-specific playbooks on LinkedIn and YouTube. His experience on the operator side gives him credibility that most content marketers lack.
