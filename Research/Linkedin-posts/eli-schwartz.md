## Post 3 — [April 18,2026]

[Most SEO teams are chasing the wrong wins.

Big technical projects feel productive, but without a clear prioritization system, you're just busy.

The real leverage is knowing what NOT to work on.

That's why I think the THRICE framework is one of the most underrated tools in SEO. It gives you a standardized way to score any initiative so you can defend your choices with data, not gut feel.

Here's what makes it work:
- Every project gets scored on the same criteria
- You can show your reasoning openly to stakeholders
- Lower-scoring projects get dropped transparently
- You walk into meetings with a defensible position
]
